# Business Requirements

## Current Business Challenges

Skyline Real Estate Management currently faces several operational and financial challenges:

1. Manual processing of vendor invoices.
2. Delays in customer payment tracking.
3. Difficulty monitoring outstanding receivables.
4. Time-consuming account reconciliations.
5. Limited visibility into property-level financial performance.
6. Complex month-end closing activities.
7. Lack of centralized financial reporting.

## Business Requirements

The company requires an integrated SAP solution capable of:

## Accounts Payable

* Managing vendor master records.
* Processing vendor invoices.
* Tracking outstanding vendor balances.
* Executing vendor payments.

## Accounts Receivable

* Managing customer master records.
* Recording rental income.
* Tracking customer outstanding balances.
* Monitoring AR Aging.

## General Ledger

* Recording all financial transactions.
* Maintaining chart of accounts.
* Supporting financial statement preparation.

## Asset Accounting

* Managing building and equipment assets.
* Tracking asset acquisition and depreciation.

## Bank Accounting

* Recording incoming and outgoing payments.
* Performing bank reconciliations.

## Reporting

* Balance Sheet
* Profit and Loss Statement
* Vendor Reports
* Customer Reports
* AR Aging Reports
* Financial Closing Reports

## Project Success Criteria

The implementation will be considered successful if financial processes are automated, reporting accuracy improves, reconciliations are completed efficiently, and management receives timely financial information for decision-making.
