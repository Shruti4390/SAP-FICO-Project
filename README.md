# SAP FICO End-to-End Business Process Simulation

## Company
Skyline Real Estate Management

## Industry
Real Estate & Property Management

## Project Objective
This project demonstrates the implementation of SAP FICO for Skyline Real Estate Management, a real estate company managing commercial and residential properties. The objective is to streamline and automate financial operations by integrating key accounting processes, while improving financial accuracy, enhancing reporting capabilities, strengthening internal controls, and reducing manual accounting efforts.

## SAP Modules Covered
- Financial Accounting (FI)
- Accounts Payable
- Accounts Receivable
- Asset Accounting
- General Ledger
- Bank Accounting
- Cost Center Accounting

## System
SAP S/4HANA
