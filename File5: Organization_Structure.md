# Organization Structure

## Overview

Skyline Real Estate Management operates through multiple functional departments that work together to manage leasing operations, property maintenance, vendor payments, customer billing, and financial reporting. SAP FICO integrates these departments into a single financial system.

## Organizational Departments

**Finance Department**

* General Ledger Accounting
* Financial Reporting
* Month-End Closing
* Bank Reconciliation

**Accounts Payable Team**

* Vendor Master Management
* Invoice Processing
* Vendor Payments

**Accounts Receivable Team**

* Customer Billing
* Lease Revenue Recording
* Payment Collection
* AR Aging Monitoring

**Property Management Team**

* Lease Administration
* Property Maintenance Coordination
* Tenant Management

**Asset Management Team**

* Building Asset Management
* Capital Expenditure Tracking
* Depreciation Monitoring

Integration with SAP

All departments share a centralized SAP S/4HANA system, ensuring real-time financial data, standardized processes, and improved operational efficiency across the organization.
