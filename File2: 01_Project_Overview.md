#Project Overview

##Business Background

Skyline Real Estate Management owns and manages multiple residential and commercial properties. The company generates revenue through property leasing and rental operations while incurring expenses related to property maintenance, utilities, vendor services, and administrative activities.

As the business grows, managing financial transactions through manual processes becomes challenging. Delays in reconciliations, fragmented reporting, and limited visibility into financial performance can impact decision-making.

To address these challenges, Skyline has decided to implement SAP S/4HANA FICO as an integrated financial management solution.

##Purpose of SAP FICO Implementation

The SAP FICO solution will help Skyline manage its financial operations in a centralized system by automating accounting transactions, improving data accuracy, and providing real-time financial visibility.

##The implementation will support:

* Vendor Invoice Processing
* Customer Billing and Receivables
* Lease Revenue Accounting
* Bank Transactions
* Fixed Asset Management
* Financial Reporting
* Month-End Closing Activities

##Expected Business Benefits

* Improved financial accuracy
* Reduced manual accounting effort
* Faster month-end closing
* Better audit compliance
* Real-time financial reporting
* Enhanced operational efficiency
