## Master Data

# Overview

Master Data represents the permanent business information required to process financial transactions within SAP S/4HANA. Accurate master data ensures consistency, reporting accuracy, and efficient transaction processing.

**General Ledger Master Data**

The General Ledger contains accounts used to record all financial transactions, including rental revenue, maintenance expenses, bank balances, depreciation, accounts payable, and accounts receivable.

**Vendor Master Data**

Vendor master records store information for contractors, maintenance service providers, utility companies, and other suppliers that provide services to Skyline Real Estate Management.

**Customer Master Data**

Customer master records maintain tenant and commercial client information used for lease billing, rent collection, and receivable management.

**Asset Master Data**

Asset master records are maintained for buildings, office equipment, furniture, and other fixed assets owned by Skyline Real Estate Management.

**Cost Centers**

Cost Centers are created to monitor expenses across different business functions, such as Administration, Property Maintenance, Finance, and Operations.

**Business Partner Concept**

SAP S/4HANA uses the Business Partner approach to centrally maintain customer and vendor information, enabling standardized master data management across financial processes.

Business Benefits

Well-maintained master data improves financial accuracy, reduces duplicate records, supports reliable reporting, and enables efficient end-to-end business processing.
